Part of our scientific vision in systematically identifying and prioritising targets involves increasing the wealth of data that provides causal links between targets and diseases in our focus therapeutic areas. Our ambitious projects harness new technologies in naturally occurring or synthetic genetics or in high-throughput, relevant, cellular models and those that have the potential to yield target lists at large-scale. Our projects ideally study genome-wide, whole-pathway or mechanistically related gene sets.

We are committed to publicly releasing our data. Below is a summary of data we have released and links to where it can be accessed.
